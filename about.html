Cogent has some pretty simple principles

We treat each other well.

We choose to be honest and open, to collaborate, and to be respectful and fair. These values are really important to who we are and how we act.

Be real

We avoid buzzwords, cut out noise, distill to the real need and get on with it. We design, build and iterate to get results. We deliver remarkable products. If you like fancy slide decks full of theories that never get to market, you will be disappointed.

Learning more.

We’re good at what we do, but the world shifts fast. That’s why we are avid learners who debate, reflect, read, participate and experiment. We commit to learning by spending a day each month together to explore new things.

Life outside work matters.

We love what we do, and life outside work hours counts too. The standard work week is - just that. We plan our projects to set ourselves up for success, not for a frenzied error-prone sprint to the finish line.

Don't see your role here? We're always looking to meet new and smart people.


Drop us a line

If you need some help with a project and this sounds good to you, let's chat.

We'll listen to your goals, ask questions, offer ideas and be up-front on our fit to do the job.
