Inspire Cogent to new heights with your business-savvy and people motivation skills.

Cogent is a company with places to go, and it's time for us to have a dedicated CEO at the helm.


We're a small Melbourne-based business with a proven delivery focused team that:

  • Provides product services to create leading web and mobile applications for customers such as Melbourne Business School and The Alfred Hospital;
  • Offers technical consulting to a variety of leading digital clients such as Red Bubble, Hooroo and REA Group; and
  • Invests in internal and partnership startups to build future revenue streams.

Vital to our continued success is a focus on our people and culture, and top-notch commercial rigour. We're after a business-savvy and entrepreneurial CEO, with creative and motivating people leadership skills. You'll naturally set about growing and diversifying the team, implementing smarter business management practices, driving initiatives to build a more creative and enjoyable workplace, and instrumenting changes to help us reach our longer term business goals.


The look of success is:

  • A scaled up Cogent business, where the team feel supported, stimulated and inspired in their day-to-day work.
  • Substantial year on year growth in revenue of product services, and of sustainable, scalable income from new Cogent startups.

Key responsibilities are to:

  • Help to set and lead the strategic direction for Cogent.
  • Drive commercial success, toward company goals.
  • Develop a thriving company culture: a stimulating and enjoyable place to work.
  • Build awareness and engagement with customers and communities.

Here's hoping you match up to this key experience list:

  • Well honed leadership style, capable of building high-performing teams, creating passion, positive energy and forward movement.
  • Multiple successes in commercial leadership, including of relevant businesses.
  • Rich experience in digital product delivery, including to be well versed in product management, design and development.
  • Entrepreneurial successes under the belt, ideally in successful digital startups.

You'll be frank by habit; prefer to have a go and adapt than worry about failure; like saying "yes, and" to new ideas; collaborate often; and enjoy walking out the door at a reasonable time to set a genuine life-centric example.
